The Mission of ReturningTenTribes.Org:

Overall, this site is to facilitate the gathering and networking of the prophesied "Remnant of Israel" who will return first to the God of Israel in "the latter days."  It gives a working definition of the biblically defined small group, "The Remnant," whose function is an intrinsic, necessary and vital component in the Divine plan of the regathering of all the Tribes of Israel. It seeks to recruit members into the "Construction Batallion" i.e. The Seabees of the Mighty God, and the establishment of associated local assemblies for the Returning Ten Tribes, (RTT) in order to further the Divine Plan for Israel’s repentant Ten Tribes to return to their God and to their Covenant–related identity and obligations.  This site does not provide a full, comprehensive definition of the so-called Lost/Exiled "Ten Tribes of Israel."  Please see links for that on the recommended links page here .  

This site is third in a series of consecutive learning designed for the pioneers of "The Returning Ten Tribes of Israel." For an adequate understanding and preparation to be partakers of this website's mission, it is highly recommended and indeed will be required of all prospective enlistees to read and study in specified order specific contents of the previous two websites in the series: http://uniteourheart.com, http://shaar-israel.org.
