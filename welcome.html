"A remnant of Israel, a remnant shall return." (Isaiah 10:20-21)

ReturningTenTribes.Org

This site’s mission is to facilitate the gathering and networking of the prophesied "Remnant of Israel" who will return early to the God of Israel in “the latter days.”  It gives a working definition of the biblically defined small group whose function is an intrinsic and vital component in the regathering of the Ten Tribes of Israel.  It seeks the establishment of networks of associated local assemblies for the Returning Ten Tribes in order to further the Divine Plan for Israel’s repentant Ten Tribes to return to their God and to their Covenant–related identity and obligations.  It does not provide a full comprehensive definition on the so-called “Lost” Ten Tribes of Israel in history and prophecy. For that please see http://www.britam.org and http://www.Hebrewnations.com.
