Orientation to Bootcamp


Some may ask, "Why is there a "military theme" throughout this site? Is such a warlike theme "appropriate" for a site which has to do with Biblical orientation whose ultimate goal is peace?"

This is an appropriate question to ask by many Biblically oriented persons, especially by those who are committed to the regathering of the Tribes of Israel. The main reasons for a military theme for the RegatheringTenTribes.org are contained in the inspired wisdom of King Solomon:

To every thing there is a season, and a time to every purpose under the heaven: A time to be born, and a time to die; a time to plant, and a time to pluck up that which is planted; A time to kill, and a time to heal; a time to break down, and a time to build up; — Ecc. 3:1-3 KJV


This key verse contains all the essential activities that a military operation does. These are also all necessary tasks that the Almighty’s Remnant is required to do. Here is a short correlation of Solomon’s observation of the scope of human activities (everything) and the assigned specially purposed “holy tasks” for the Remnant to do.

Purpose:

The LORD is a man of war, The LORD is His name. ְהוָה אִישׁ מִלְחָמָה יְהוָה שְׁמו

— Ex. 15:3 KJV

The God of Israel calls himself “A Man of War.” The very purpose, the singular raison d’etre of the Remnant is to be a “saving” Remnant in the “service” of the same God of Israel who went to war in order to save Israel from slavish imprisonment in idolatrous Egypt. I am the LORD thy God, who brought thee out of the land of Egypt, out of the house of bondage. Thou shalt have no other gods before Me.

— Ex. 20:3 KJV


The “saving Remnant” of this “Man of War” is called to serve as God’s appointed special forces, “armed” with the power of God’s Spirit to carry out essential parts of His plan for saving Israel in times of existential crises. Yes, it is a “service” just as the armed forces of our countries are a “service” with multiple tasks. Yet, as “government issues” (GIs) of the Almighty, the overall purpose of His Remnant is to defend and promote the safety of the people whose God-given values and government they are sworn to uphold. They built beach and interior land access, roads, bridges, air strips, fortifications and communication lines in theaters of war all under enemy fire. On this site the ReturningTenTribes.org “Remnant of the Almighty” in our time is alternately referred to as the “Seabees of the Almighty.” They are to be preparatory troops in the process of the Redemption of Israel.
